How to play

You are betting on the result of a 100 sided die roll. The result of the die roll is either a low roll range (1-49) or a high roll range (50-100).

If the number you submit is within the result range; low range numbers are (1-49), high range numbers are (50-100) you are paid 1.9 to 1 instantly.

If the number you submit is outside the result range you are not paid.

How is the result decided?

((SeedA + SeedB + SeedC) / (2 ^ 256 / 100) ) +1 = result

SeedA = Is set before you place your bet, and we cannot change it. Basically; when you make a bet the contract will retrieve and store the blockhash of the parent block relative to the block you made your bet on. The contract then uses that as our SeedA number. See 'provably fair' below for more details. 1st dice rolled.

SeedB = Your number choice 1-100. 2nd dice rolled.

SeedC = Hash of block in a future time. 3rd dice rolled.

Before any bets are placed, we have already generated a number from a previous block hash. This is SeedA.

Once you submit your bet (SeedB), the Etheroll contract waits for a mumber of new Ethereum blocks to be mined and we use a future block hash from which SeedC generated, therefore SeedC is impossible for us or you to know prior to the submission of the bet as the block did not yet exist.

Etheroll pays 1.9 to 1 if the number you submitted is within the resulting range, either 'high' or 'low'.

The chance of the dice roll being either high or low is 50/50.

Provably Fair

SeedA is generated by a block hash previous to your bet. By the time you make your bet, SeedA was already generated by the Ethereum Blockchain, we pull the block hash information from that block to use as SeedA.

You can check the block number and compare its block hash to sha3 using any good block explorer. This will confirm that we did indeed use the number we said we did (a number that already existed before you made your bet). We provide the sha3 encrypted version of SeedA ('SHA3 of SeedA'). This is to prove that we do not (and could not - mathematical impossibility) change SeedA after you submit your bet. This means Etheroll will always have picked a number before you pick yours.
