The Game

You are betting on the result of a 100 sided die roll. The result of the die roll is either a low roll range (1-49) or a high roll range (50-100).

If the number you submit is within the result range; low range numbers are (1-49), high range numbers are (50-100) you are paid 1.9 to 1 instantly.

If the number you submit is outside the result range you are not paid.

How is the result decided?

((SeedA + SeedB + SeedC) / (2 ^ 256 / 100) ) +1 = result

SeedA = SeedA is set before you place your bet, and we cannot change it. See 'provably fair' below for more details. 1st dice rolled.

SeedB = Your number choice 1-100. 2nd dice rolled.

SeedC = Hash of block in a future time. 3rd dice rolled.

Before any bets are placed, we have already generated a number from a previous block hash. This is SeedA.

Once you submit your bet (SeedB), the Etherflipr contract waits for a mumber of new Ethereum blocks to be mined and we use a future block hash from which SeedC generated, therefore SeedC is impossible for us or you to know prior to the submission of the bet as the block did not yet exist.

Etherflipr pays 1.9 to 1 if the number you submitted is within the resulting range, either 'high' or 'low'.

The chance of the dice roll being either high or low is 50/50.

Provably Fair

We provide the sha3 encrypted version of SeedA ('SeedA Hash') before and after each bet for you to check, to prove that we do not (and could not - mathematical impossibility) change SeedA after you submit your bet.
